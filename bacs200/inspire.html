This is my HERO: Ruth Bader Ginsburg

March 15,1933 - September 18,2020

Who is Ruth Bader Ginsburg?

RBG was an American lawyer and servered as a judge on the Supreme Court of the United States from 1993 until her death in 2020.

She inspires me and is a role model. RBG never gives up. She has been fighting Gender equality since she was young. Even till her death bed, she was still fighting.

RBG has been through many challenges, from her family to schools. However, she does not give up. RBG was a brilliant Supreme Court Justice.

Despite her serious health problem during 2019-20, she fought for her values and beliefs.

She fought for women’s right.

She is the founding mother of our nation’s gender equality jurisprudence.

She overcome discrimination and helped others do the same.

I also have the same beliefs and values. Becasue she fought till her last breath, it inspired me to also keep going.

Some of my favorite Quotes By RBG

"Fight for the things that you care about, but do it in a way that will lead others to join you."

"A gebder line ... helps to keep women not on a pedestal, but in a cage."

"Don't be distracted by emotions like anger, envy, resentment. These just zap energy and waste time."

"If you have a caring life partner, you help the other person when that person needs it. I have a life partner who thought my work was as important as his, and that made all the difference for me."

"I would like to be remembered as someone who used whatever talent she had to do her work to the very best of her ability."
