Social-Emotional Health

Teaching and Learning from home have taken their tool on teachers, student and parents. Stress levels have significantly increased since COVID. Students' social-emotional well-being is impacted. Having to talk to screen alll day long and not being able to intereact with friends has increased their emotional health. Some students have given up. Even if they start their day strong, they will later meltdown. It has also impacts focus, memory, and overall learning.

At a time like this, it is important to start the day by sharing how they are feeling. It helps to talk about it. It also helped all of us to take a deep breath, and relese whta was bothering us. So, that we can move on with our day in a better frame of mind.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a hot topic in education technology. AI helps gather data to guide instruction and accelerates learner growth by adapting to individual student needs.

However, that also takes away intelligence. Student will never be able to learn if all they know is taking short cut and knowing that they don't have to try.

AI in a ways is helpful. AI allows teachers to automate tasks like grading fill-in-the-blank and multiple-choice questions, tutors can support students by providing feedback and monitoring their progress in real-time.
